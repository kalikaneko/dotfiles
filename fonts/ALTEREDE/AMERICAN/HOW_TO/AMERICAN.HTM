Suggestions for using American Spirit!" STF:

For Programs that support Kerning:

  1. First, set the characters “a”, “space”, and “b”. (The space will separate the characters to keep them from kerning)
  2. Colorize the Union and the Stripes
  3. Remove the space, and the Flag will kern together.

For programs that do not support Kerning:

  1. First, set the characters “a”, “space”, and “c”. (The space will separate the characters to keep them from kerning)
  2. Colorize the Union and the Stripes
  3. Remove the space, and the Flag will kern together.

For printing these flags at smaller sizes and resolution, we suggest using the “g” character, as the shape of the stars will look better in smaller sizes, and will kern to all the variations.

Other characters can be imported in a vector illustration program such as FreeHand(tm) or Illustrator(tm) and converted to paths for ease in colorizing.
